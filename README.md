# FMCG Sales & Profit Performance Analysis

## Objective
To identify underperforming regions, top products 
and profit leakage areas for an FMCG company 
across 5 global regions from 2023 to 2025.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel — Data Cleaning & Analysis
- Power BI — Dashboard & Visualization

## Dataset
- 18,240 rows of FMCG sales data
- Period: 2023–2025
- 5 Regions | 5 Product Categories | 4 Sales Channels

## Key Insights
- Europe leads total profit at $1.14M
- Personal Care has highest margin at 25.98%
- Wholesale dominates with 40% of total profit
- Revenue grew 9.2% from 2023 to 2025
- December consistently peaks across all years
- Higher discounts maintain better margins

## Recommendations
- Invest more in Personal Care — highest margin
- Review Online channel strategy — lowest profit
- Leverage December seasonality for promotions
- Expand Wholesale partnerships
